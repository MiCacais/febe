# 🛡️ FeBe Compliance Job Description Badges

If your hiring pipeline is fully aligned with the FeBe (For Engineers By Engineers) methodology, copy and paste one of the snippets below into the very top of your job posting.

Placing this at the top of your job description instantly sets you apart in the talent market, signaling to pragmatic builders that you value execution over performance art.

---

## Option 1: Markdown Badge (Recommended for GitHub, Notion, Greenhouse)

This option displays a clean visual shield badge followed by a concise summary.

```markdown
🛡️ [![FeBe Compliant](https://img.shields.io/badge/Hiring-FeBe%20Compliant-brightgreen)](#)

**This role uses the FeBe Hiring Methodology.**

We filter for real-world execution, not interview showmanship.

Max 3 rounds total • Exactly one focused technical evaluation • No endless gauntlets • Respect for candidate time
```

---

## Option 2: HTML Blockquote (Great for LinkedIn, Lever, or Custom Career Sites)

A formatted blockquote that stands out visually on traditional job boards.

```html
<blockquote>
  <strong>🛡️ FeBe Certified Hiring Process</strong>
  <p>
    We believe in hiring engineers for their ability to build.
    This pipeline is strictly limited to <strong>3 rounds maximum</strong> and includes exactly
    <strong>one focused technical evaluation</strong> tailored to the role.
    No endless gauntlets. No abstract brain teasers. Full respect for your time.
  </p>
</blockquote>
```

---

## Option 3: Plain Text / Minimalist (For Rigid ATS Platforms)

If your job board doesn't support rich formatting or Markdown, use this version.

```text
🛡️ FEBE COMPLIANT PIPELINE

Max 3 Rounds • 1 Technical Evaluation

We don't run 5-round gauntlets or ask abstract algorithmic riddles.

This role uses the FeBe (For Engineers By Engineers) framework:
an intentional, highly focused hiring process that respects your time
and evaluates real-world delivery over interview performance.
```
