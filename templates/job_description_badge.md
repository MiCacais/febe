# 🛡️ FeBe Compliance Job Description Badges

If your hiring pipeline is fully compliant with the FeBe (For Engineers By Engineers) methodology, copy and paste one of the snippets below into the very top of your job posting. 

Placing this at the top of your job description instantly sets you apart in the talent market, signaling to elite, pragmatic builders that you value execution over performance art.

---

## Option 1: Markdown Badge (Recommended for GitHub/Notion/Greenhouse)

This option displays a clean, visual shield badge followed by a concise summary.

```markdown
🛡️ [![FeBe Compliant](https://img.shields.io/badge/Hiring-FeBe%20Compliant-brightgreen)](#) **This role uses the FeBe Hiring Methodology.** 
We filter for real-world execution, not interview showmanship. That means: Max 3 rounds total | Exactly ONE focused technical track | No arbitrary behavioral traps. We respect your time.

## Option 2: HTML Blockquote (Great for LinkedIn, Lever, or Custom Career Sites)

A beautifully formatted blockquote that stands out visually on traditional job boards.

```html
<blockquote>
  <strong>🛡️ FeBe Certified Hiring Process</strong>
  <p>We believe in hiring engineers for their ability to build, not their ability to pass theater auditions. This pipeline is strictly limited to <strong>maximum 3 rounds</strong> and features exactly <strong>ONE focused technical evaluation</strong> tailored to the job. No endless gauntlets, no abstract brain teasers, and full respect for your personal boundaries.</p>
</blockquote>
```

## Option 3: Plain Text / Minimalist (For rigid ATS platforms)

If your job board doesn't support rich formatting or markdown, use this high-impact text block.

🛡️ FEBE COMPLIANT PIPELINE (Max 3 Rounds | 1 Tech Evaluation)
We don't run 5-round gauntlets or ask abstract algorithmic riddles. This role uses the FeBe (For Engineers By Engineers) framework: an intentional, highly focused 3-step alignment process that respects your time and tests for real-world delivery.
